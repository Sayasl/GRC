# Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) Project

A practical Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) project designed for small organizations implementing an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned with **ISO 27001** principles.

This repository provides a structured approach to managing information security risks through documented policies, risk tracking, compliance monitoring, and reporting.


## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how a small company can establish foundational GRC processes and maintain security governance documentation.

The project includes:

- Information Security Risk Register
- Risk Assessment Methodology
- Risk Register Checklist
- Security Policy
- Dashboard for Risk Monitoring
- Compliance Reporting


## Objectives

- Establish a repeatable information security risk management process
- Identify and assess business and cybersecurity risks
- Track remediation actions and ownership
- Measure security control effectiveness
- Support ISO 27001 readiness and audit preparation
- Improve visibility through dashboard reporting

## Contents Of The Project
- Security Policy

The purpose of this Information Security Policy is to establish the principles, responsibilities,
and controls necessary to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the Company’s
information assets and information systems.
- Risk Registry

The file includes key fields such as:

1) Risk ID: Unique identifier for each risk
2) Asset/Process: System, data, or business area affected
3) Risk Description: Summary of the identified risk
4) Threat & Vulnerability: Cause and weakness associated with the risk
5) Likelihood & Impact: Risk evaluation criteria
6) Risk Score: Overall risk rating
7) Risk Owner: Person responsible for managing the risk
8) Existing Controls: Current protections in place
9) Treatment Plan: Actions to reduce or manage risk
10) ISO 27001 Reference: Related security control mapping
- Risk Registry Checklist

Risk Registry Checklist designed for a small company to manage and track information security risks, control implementation, and compliance activities.

File Contents:
1) Dashboard: High-level overview of security and risk metrics (total risks, open risks, high risks, average risk score, and status tracking).
2) Risk Checklist: Central risk register
3) Control Verification: Tracks whether ISO 27001 security controls are implemented, identifies gaps, and records remediation actions and priorities.
4) Review Sign-Off: Maintains periodic review history, reviewer approvals, updates, and management sign-off records.
